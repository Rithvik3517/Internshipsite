Internship Roadmap

Congratulations on joining the Sample internship program! Here's how to successfully complete your internship:

Step 1: Orientation

Attend the orientation session to understand the program structure and expectations.

Step 2: Skill Building

Participate in skill-building workshops and online courses to enhance your knowledge.

Step 3: Project Assignment

Receive a project assignment aligned with your chosen domain.

Step 4: Project Development

Work on your project, collaborate with mentors, and apply what you've learned.

Step 5: Review and Iteration

Submit your project for review, receive feedback, and iterate on improvements.

Step 6: Final Presentation

Present your project to the team and demonstrate your achievements.

Step 7: Completion

Congratulations! You've successfully completed the internship program.
